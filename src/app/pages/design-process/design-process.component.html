My Design Process

Design Research

I identify my users by finding out who, what, where, when and how they are in relation to the product or service. It gives me an insight into the problems we're trying to solve. I generate quite a number of interview questions for my users, and learn from the response.

Generate Ideas

After identifying these problems, I come up with ideas to create meaningful discussions with the team.

Define all ideas

In this phase, I make sense of all the ideas collected by using the post-it approach. This helps the team make decisions on which idea solves our user's problems.

Prototyping

Wireframes are designed and I can demonstrate the idea to colleagues, stackholders and a user-group. This phase helps me to answer specific questions, and get feedback from people.
