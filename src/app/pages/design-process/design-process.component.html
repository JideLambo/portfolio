Design Research

I identify my users and try to find their needs. I also try to learn of the context of their problem. It gives me an insight into the problems we're trying to solve.

Generate Ideas

After identifying these problems, I come up with ideas by observing, interviewing, tracking and surveys.

Define all ideas

In this phase, I make sense of all the ideas collected by using the wireframes or low-fi designs. This helps the team make decisions on which idea solves our user's problems.

Prototyping

Prototypes are designed and I can demonstrate the idea to colleagues, stackholders and a user-group. This phase helps me answer specific questions, and get feedback from people.
